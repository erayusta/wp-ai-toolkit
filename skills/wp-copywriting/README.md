# WP Copywriting

> Craft SEO headlines, meta descriptions, and featured snippet content

## What This Skill Covers

- Writing compelling SEO titles within character limits
- Crafting meta descriptions that improve click-through rates
- Structuring content to win featured snippets and People Also Ask
- Creating power words and emotional triggers for headlines

## Example Usage

Ask your AI assistant:

```
> "Write SEO-optimized meta titles and descriptions for my top 5 pages on wordpress.test"
```

```
Page: /wordpress-speed-optimization/
  Title (58 chars): "WordPress Speed Optimization: 15 Proven Tips for 2026"
```
